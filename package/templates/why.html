Because early identification and treatment of mental illness are of vital importance:


  • In the United States, 46% of adults will experience a mental illness during their lifetime.
  • In many cases, a mental illness goes undiagnosed.
  • Untreated mental health conditions can result in
    • shrinkage of key brain areas
    • unnecessary disability, unemployment,
    • substance abuse, homelessness,
    • inappropriate incarceration,
    • suicide, & poor quality of life
  • Untreated mental illnesses cost about $100 billion a year in lost productivity.
