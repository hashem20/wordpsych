The current WordPsych application has been trained by data from reddit.com. First, we searched for reddit groups (subreddits) specific to a mental health problem, like r/depression and r/anxiety. Next, the members of these groups were identified by their reddit IDs. Lastly, we searched for posts/comments by these authors all over the reddit (other subreddits than the original mental health related ones) and gathered around a billion pieces of text.

Some of the labels are aggregate of two or more subgroups. For example, personality disorders have been grouped into the three well-known conventional Clusters A, B, and C, each of which includes several diagnoses.
